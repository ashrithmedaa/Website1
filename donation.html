Donation Guidelines

How to donate to Flanders Methodist Thrift Shop

Thank you for supporting our thrift shop ministry. Your donations help fund church outreach, local missions, and community service.

Where to donate

Donate items during shop hours or drop them in the designated donation shed and tents any time during daylight hours.

Donation types we accept

Clean clothing, shoes, handbags, jewelry, household items, books, décor, small appliances, and gently used seasonal goods are appreciated.

Donation receipt

If you need a signed donation receipt, please bring your items during open hours so a volunteer can assist you.

Items we cannot accept

We are a small volunteer-powered ministry and cannot accept the following items:

  • TVs (working or not)
  • Propane tanks
  • Chemicals, flammable liquids, oil, hazardous materials
  • Large furniture (must fit in a car)
  • Tires
  • Paint and mattresses
  • Wet clothing
  • Large appliances or non-working air conditioners
  • Construction debris or obvious garbage

Please do not donate items that damage our volunteers’ ability to serve the community or that require costly disposal.

Quick donation tips

  • Pack clothing, handbags, shoes, and jewelry in tied plastic bags to protect them from weather.
  • Place clothing in the donation shed so the items stay dry and clean.
  • Use the donation tents for household goods and other items.

We are not a recycling center or dump. Donations help our ministry only when they are usable and safe for volunteers to handle.
