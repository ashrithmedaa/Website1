Background & Mission

Thriving as a charitable ministry in Flanders

The Flanders Methodist Thrift Shop is a long-running charitable resale store operated by Flanders United Methodist Church. It provides affordable goods to the public while supporting church programs, humanitarian projects, and Methodist mission work locally and abroad.

Our mission is to promote reuse and sustainability through low-cost shopping that benefits donors, shoppers, and the broader community.

Community-first values

Staffed by volunteers, the thrift shop is also a social hub for local residents and a source of support for neighbors in need.

Stewardship in action

Proceeds fund church outreach, community missions, and volunteer-driven service initiatives throughout Morris County and beyond.

Daily donation drop-off

Donations are accepted daily during daylight hours through designated sheds and tents on site.

Our Church

Flanders United Methodist Church

The thrift shop is an outreach ministry of Flanders United Methodist Church. For worship, community events, and church updates, visit the main church website.

Weekly worship is held Sundays at 10:00 AM, with online participation available at the same time. Bible study meets Tuesdays at 7:30 PM, and the Friday Prayer Meeting is held via Zoom at 7:30 PM.

Operations & Offerings

What you can find inside

From clothing and accessories to household goods and seasonal finds, our shop keeps prices low so every visit is rewarding.

Clothing & Accessories

Racks of gently used clothing, shoes, handbags, jewelry, and vintage finds for every season.

Home + Gift Shop

Household items, décor, kitchenware, seasonal decorations, and small treasures at great value.

Books & Media

Books, puzzles, and media picked to inspire reading, learning, and easy gifts.

Bag Sales & Specials

Occasional bag sales encourage inventory turnover and make it easy to take home more for less.

Community Role

A volunteer-powered ministry with a local heartbeat

Many of our volunteers are retirees and church members who love serving the community. The shop supports local service initiatives and embodies compassionate stewardship for neighbors in need.
